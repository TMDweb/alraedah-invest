How to read this page

Seven lifecycle phases, ordered from earliest teaser to final funded celebration. Each phase gives you three forms of the same copy so you can pick the right length for the channel you are publishing to.

Standard form
The atomic elements: kicker (label above the headline), headline, body, chips (short info badges), CTA. Use this for any social composition where a designer or template assembles the layout.
LinkedIn long caption
A 2 to 4 sentence expansion for the post body text on LinkedIn. Use when the post has a caption above or below the image, not only a hero graphic.
Story short form
Two to three line compressed version for Instagram story or WhatsApp status. When space is tight, this is what fits.

Variable slots appear as {{category}}, {{city}}, and so on. They are filled in at publish time per opportunity. The full list is in the Variables section below.

English and Arabic sit side by side per phase. The Arabic register follows the foundational-doc voice (conversational Saudi Arabic), which the client has directed us to treat as authoritative.

{{funded_hours}}Hours from open to full funding (e.g. 14).

Production notes

  • Legal review. Every post containing a number (rate, tenor, raise, funded percentage, investor count) needs per-opportunity legal sign-off before publishing. Track in asset-index.csv from B2.
  • No hashtags by default. Matches the decision taken in B2. If a specific channel requires them, add at publish time, not in the template.
  • No references to app.alraedah.sa. The prebrand templates point to this URL; it is out of date and must be dropped. Use alraedah.sa/invest or the specific opportunity URL.
  • Footer compliance line, if a channel or regulator requires an inline disclosure: English: Licensed by SAMA. Arabic: خاضعة لرقابة البنك المركزي السعودي. The foundational doc's "مضمونة" wording is prohibited per SAMA and is not used in any post here.
  • Arabic register. These posts follow the foundational doc's conversational Saudi Arabic (the authoritative source for product narrative). If the client prefers more formal MSA, swap specific verbs and pronouns (اللي → التي, معاك → معك, etc.); the numeric story stays identical.
  • Source of truth. This page and copy-template.docx should stay synchronised. When one edits, the other follows.
