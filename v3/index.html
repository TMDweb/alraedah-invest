Is my capital guaranteed?

No. Returns are target-based and capital is subject to default, delay, cancellation, and liquidity risk.

Why monthly payouts?

Scheduled monthly payments help investors see capital returning over time instead of waiting until the end of the full period.

What gives confidence?

Alraedah reviews opportunities, SAMA licensing applies, Shariah review applies, and Alraedah may co-invest up to 25% alongside investors.

What happens when no opportunity is open?
