Questions investors ask first.

Short answers to the questions investors ask first.

How does Alraedah review opportunities?

Opportunities are reviewed by Alraedah before listing, with the return, tenor, amount, and monthly distribution structure shown upfront.

Is the return really 7%?

It is a 7% effective target return. Because you receive capital plus target return monthly, the amount still invested decreases over time.

Why would I choose this over a 4% bank example?

The bank example pays at the end. Alraedah pays monthly. If you reinvest those monthly distributions, the illustrative outcome can be higher.

Why monthly payouts?

Monthly payouts make your money available during the year. You can keep the cash or reinvest it.

What is the minimum investment?

You can start from SAR 1,000.

Is Alraedah Invest regulated?

Yes. Alraedah is licensed and regulated by the Saudi Central Bank (SAMA), and opportunities are reviewed for Shariah compliance.
