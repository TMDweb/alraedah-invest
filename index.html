Risk, in plain language

What can go wrong, and how we manage it.

Debt crowdfunding is an investment, and investments carry risk. We don't hide it. Here is what you need to know.

Default risk

A business may fail to repay. If that happens, your return may be partial, delayed, or in some cases unrecoverable.

Delay risk

Monthly distributions can be delayed if a business hits short-term cash-flow stress, even when it ultimately repays.

Cancellation risk

An opportunity can be cancelled before it closes. Your capital is returned without return.

Liquidity risk

Your capital is committed for the tenor you pick. There is no secondary market at launch; you cannot exit early.

How we manage risk:

  • Every opportunity is underwritten by Alraedah's 10+ years of investment experience.
  • Alraedah co-invests up to 25% of every deal, so our interests are aligned with yours.
  • Shariah-committee-supervised structures across every product.
  • SAMA regulation, with a compliance and complaint-handling process.

Frequently asked

The questions investors ask us first.

About your return

Is the return really 7%? Why does the profit on SAR 10,000 come out to SAR 383, not SAR 700?

The 7% is an effective return, not a flat yearly rate. Because you receive part of your capital back every month, the amount that is actively earning inside the platform gradually decreases over the year. Your full benefit from the 7% target shows up only when you reinvest each monthly payout; then your profit grows to approximately SAR 723 on SAR 10,000 over 12 months.

If the bank gives me SAR 400 and you give me SAR 383, why would I choose you?

With a bank deposit, your money is locked for the full year. You cannot use it. With Alraedah Invest, you receive approximately SAR 865 every month, which you can spend, save, or reinvest. If you reinvest, your final amount is approximately SAR 10,723, more than the bank. The monthly liquidity is the real advantage.

What does "effective return" mean, and how does it differ from a flat rate?

A flat rate is calculated as if your full capital stays locked for the entire period. An effective return accounts for when you receive your money. Because payouts come monthly, your capital returns to you quickly and can keep working, what we call "your money stays moving."

About the mechanics

Do I have to reinvest my monthly payouts?

No. Reinvestment is optional. You can withdraw each monthly payout to your bank account and use it as cash. But if you reinvest, you compound your return, reinvested payouts themselves begin earning, which is what pushes the 12-month total from approximately SAR 10,383 to approximately SAR 10,723 on SAR 10,000.

How much will I receive each month?

On a SAR 10,000 investment over 12 months at a 7% target, your monthly payout is approximately SAR 865. Each payout includes a portion of your original capital plus your target return for that month. The calculator above gives you the exact number for any amount and tenor.

Safety and the platform

Is my capital guaranteed? What are the risks?

Alraedah has over 10 years of experience in the investment field. Every opportunity on the platform is underwritten by our in-house credit team, reviewed by a Shariah committee, and Alraedah co-invests up to 25% of every deal alongside you. Returns are target-based, and we work actively to protect them. See the risk section above for how risk works on the platform.

What is compounding? Can you show me?

Compounding means the returns you receive also start earning returns. Example: the first monthly payout of SAR 865 on a 12-month deal, if reinvested, begins earning for the remaining 11 months. The second payout earns for 10 months. And so on. By the end of the year, the difference is visible: approximately SAR 723 in profit with reinvestment, compared to SAR 383 without.

What is the minimum amount? What tenors are available?

Minimum investment is SAR 1,000. Tenors available at launch: 12, 24, and 36 months, depending on the specific opportunity.
